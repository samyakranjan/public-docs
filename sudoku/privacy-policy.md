# Privacy Policy

## Sudoku

*Last updated: May 4, 2026*

---

## 1. Introduction

Samaru Labs ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Sudoku mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our App. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use the App.

## 2. Information We Collect

**Personal Information:** When you choose to sign in using Google, we collect your email address and display name as provided by Google. Sign-in is entirely optional — the App is fully usable without an account.

**Game Data:** We store your game progress, statistics, achievements, and preferences locally on your device. If you sign in with a Google account, this data is also synced to Google Cloud Firestore to enable cross-device access and backup.

We do not collect device identifiers, location data, advertising IDs, or any other personal information beyond what is described above.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

- Authenticate your identity when you sign in.
- Sync your game progress and saved data across devices when you are signed in.
- Restore your data after a reinstall when you sign in again.

We do not display advertisements, process in-app purchases, or use your data for analytics or marketing.

## 4. Third-Party Services

Our App integrates Google Firebase for authentication and data storage. Google's privacy policy applies to data processed by Firebase: [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

We do not integrate any advertising networks, analytics SDKs, or other third-party data processors.

We do not sell your personal information to any third party.

## 5. Data Retention and Deletion

Your local data remains on your device until you uninstall the App or clear app data. If you have signed in, your cloud data is stored in Google Cloud Firestore under your Google account.

You can delete your cloud data at any time from within the App: go to **Settings → Account → Delete cloud data**. This permanently removes all data associated with your account from our servers. Your local data on the device is not affected.

You may also contact us at [samaru.labs@gmail.com](mailto:samaru.labs@gmail.com) to request deletion, and we will complete it within a reasonable timeframe.

## 6. Data Security

All data transmitted between the App and Google Firebase is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS. Data at rest in Firestore is encrypted by Google's infrastructure. Access to your data is restricted to your Google account — no other user can access it.

## 7. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at [samaru.labs@gmail.com](mailto:samaru.labs@gmail.com) and we will delete it promptly.

## 8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access the personal data we hold about you.
- Request correction of inaccurate data.
- Request deletion of your data (see Section 5 above).
- Withdraw consent at any time by signing out of the App.

To exercise any of these rights, use the in-app controls in Settings → Account, or contact us at [samaru.labs@gmail.com](mailto:samaru.labs@gmail.com).

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this document. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
