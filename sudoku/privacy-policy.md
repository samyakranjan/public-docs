Samaru Labs ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Sudoku mobile application (the "App"). ## 2. Information We Collect

**Personal Information:** When you create an account or sign in using a social login provider (such as Google or Facebook), we may collect your email address and basic profile information provided by the social login service.

**Device Information:** We may automatically collect certain device-related information, including your device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and general diagnostic data. This helps us improve the App's performance and troubleshoot issues.

**Game Data:** We store your game progress, saved puzzles, and preferences locally on your device and may sync this data to our servers to enable cross-device access when you are signed in.

**Usage and Analytics Data:** We collect anonymised usage data such as session duration, feature usage patterns, and crash reports to help us understand how the App is used and to improve our services.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

- To create and manage your account and authenticate your identity.
- To sync your game progress and saved data across devices.
- To analyse usage trends, monitor performance, and improve the App.
- To display advertisements through Google AdMob.
- To process in-app purchases (such as ad removal).
- To communicate with you regarding support requests or policy updates.
